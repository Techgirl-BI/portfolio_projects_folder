Tribute to Folawe Omikunle

Honoring the impactful leadership of the former CEO of Teach For Nigeria.

Biography

Folawe Omikunle is an accomplished leader in the field of education, known for her transformative role as the CEO of Teach For Nigeria. Under her guidance, the organization has made significant strides in addressing educational inequity in Nigeria. She is passionate about ensuring that all children have access to quality education.

Achievements

  • Successfully led Teach For Nigeria to increase its reach and impact across underserved communities.
  • Advocated for educational reform and policies to improve learning outcomes in Nigeria.
  • Mentored numerous young leaders and educators, fostering a new generation of changemakers.
  • Collaborated with various stakeholders to create sustainable educational initiatives.
